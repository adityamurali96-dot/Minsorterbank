Group an Indian bank statement by counterparty. Supports ICICI, Axis, HDFC, SBI, Kotak, Canara — or any tabular Excel/CSV.

Pick your bank to skip auto-detection and improve parsing accuracy.

If Tabula collapsed the Date into the Narration cell (e.g. “04/04/25 UPI-ZERODHA…” in column A), pick “Yes” to split them apart before sorting. Requires .xlsx.

If your file is a Tabula-extracted PDF export with wrapped narration on continuation rows, pick “Yes” to merge them before sorting.
